# Product Scope Alignment

Use this skill to keep Proofound launch-safe.

## Align Before Expanding

- Compare proposed work against the locked MVP authority stack and current repo evidence.
- Preserve Proofound's proof-first hiring corridor: Proof Packs, explicit visibility, blind-by-default review, progressive reveal, and narrow individual/organization flows.
- Challenge drift into public directories, ATS/HRIS replacement, broad org-suite or BI features, social/feed behavior, hidden people ranking, unnecessary native-provider blockers, unmeasured infrastructure expansion, or production OCR/document AI without approval.

## Recommendation Shape

- Prefer the smallest valuable launch-safe option.
- If broader work is justified, split it into explicit stages with risk and verification per stage.
- Call out mismatches between docs, code, tickets, and product doctrine before changing behavior.
